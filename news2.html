Japan's Prime Minister Shigeru Ishiba and India's Prime Minister Narendra Modi agreed to deepen economic and security cooperation in Tokyo. They discussed defense collaboration, supply chain strengthening, AI, space, high-speed rail, and other technology partnerships for the next decade.
